about us

We are the nobodies collective. We are a collective dedicated to building artistic community and nurturing new works.

Each month, we gather to develop new work. Any type of new work that requires collaboration with others for the work to progress.

While traditionally this has meant gathering around a new play or screenplay draft (i.e. writers and directors spearheading projects), we want actors and other interpretive artists (i.e. folks who don’t identify as writers, directors) to remember that you have creative agency here.

If a project will help your process, it’s right for Lab.



mission statement

A few fundamental values that run through everything we do:

  • we aim to cultivate a brave space for creative exploration. midwifing stories involves risk. stories worth telling often venture into topics that are emotionally dangerous, uncomfortable, difficult; we believe it’s possible to bravely explore difficult content while caring for and respecting the artist’s well-being.

  • we believe in the transformative power of community building; we believe being in community is vital to our survival as artists.

  • leading with transparency: we believe that cultivating and equitable space is incomplete without transparency from those who hold power; the coven’s metaphorical door is always open. If you have concerns or hopes or dreams about how you want to cast/write/produce/direct, you can come to us.

  • joining us: we encourage members to give (time, talent, writing, projects, etc.) as they can, understanding that the life of a working artist is emotionally, energetically, financially, logistically demanding. come when you can, leave gently when you can’t. it’s hard out there. we strive to create a warm home where you can rest your weary feet. there’s room to stay, there’s grace to leave.

We are Nobody! Who are you? Are you Nobody, too?
