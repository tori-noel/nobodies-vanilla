about us

Hi, we’re the nobodies collective. We nurture new works for the stage and screen. We create brave spaces for new work development and building artistic community among actors, writers, directors, and producers.
Our main program at this time is Monthly Lab: a professional and supportive environment for artists to workshop new projects.



mission statement

A few fundamental values that run through everything we do:

  • Cultivate a brave space for creative exploration.

     Midwifing stories involves risk. We believe it’s possible to explore difficult content while caring for the artist’s well-being. Our goal is to foster great work, with kindness and compassion.

  • Community Building is Transformative.

    Creating community is a way to practice abundance mindset; in a competitve industry, we believe that forging community amongst our peers is vital to our survival.

  • Leading with Transparency

     Cultivating an equitable space is incomplete without transparency from those who hold power; In our practice, the coven (leadership)’s metaphorical door is always open. If you have concerns or hopes or dreams about how you want to cast/write/produce/direct, you can come to us.

  • Open Door For Artists

     We encourage artists to give (time, talent, writing, projects, etc.) as they can, understanding that the life of a working artist is emotionally, energetically, financially, logistically demanding. Come when you can, leave gently when you can’t. We are a warm home where you can rest your weary feet.
